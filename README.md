Global Electric Vehicle (EV) Market Analysis (2010 - 2023)

Project Overview:

This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the International Energy Agency's (IEA) Global EV dataset. The goal is to analyze historical adoption rates, regional dominance, and consumer preferences between pure electric (BEV) and hybrid (PHEV) powertrains to identify future market opportunities.

Tools & Libraries Used:

Python 3
Pandas: Data cleaning, boolean filtering, and mathematical aggregations (YoY growth).
Matplotlib: Data visualization and trend mapping.

Key Business Insights Discovered:

The Inflection Point: Global EV sales transitioned from linear to exponential growth starting in 2020, with a massive Year-over-Year growth spike.
Regional Dominance: Excluding global totals, China thoroughly dominated the EV market in 2023, outpacing Europe and the USA significantly.
Powertrain Preference: Consumers are heavily favoring Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) over Plug-in Hybrids (PHEVs), indicating a strong global trust in charging infrastructure.
